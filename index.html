May 21 2019
3.3V Supply and mounted TinyFPGA-BX
steve
STEVE’s new look orginal image A 3.3V supply has been added to drive the TinyFPGA-BX and other 3.3V devices. Once the power supply was tuned (tiny multiturn pot) some nail polish was painted on the screw so it does not move. The 10V rail (8 AA , NiMH) has been covered with Kapton tape to avoid accidental shorts. There is also a 5<->3.3V signal converter, so there are 8 lines that can be broken out to the other side of STEVE.
